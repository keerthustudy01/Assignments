Steps to Cook Vegetable Fried Rice

  1. Wash and chop all vegetables (carrot, beans, capsicum, peas).
  2. Heat oil in a pan and saut√© onions until golden.
  3. Add chopped vegetables and stir-fry for a few minutes.
  4. Add cooked rice and mix well.
  5. Season with salt, pepper, and soy sauce.
  6. Stir everything together and cook for 2 more minutes.
  7. Serve hot and enjoy!
  8. Optional: Garnish with chopped spring onions or coriander leaves.
